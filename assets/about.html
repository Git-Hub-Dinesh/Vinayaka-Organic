Our Story A Legacy of Pure Tradition

Vinayaga Exports was founded with a simple mission: to preserve the traditional art of jaggery making and bring pure, natural sweetness to every household. For generations, our family has been perfecting the craft of producing the finest quality jaggery.

What started as a small family business has now grown into a trusted name in Vinayaga jaggery Exports. Our commitment to quality, authenticity, and customer satisfaction has helped us build lasting relationships with clients worldwide.

"Every block of jaggery we produce carries the essence of tradition, the warmth of nature, and the promise of purity."

Traditional Jaggery-Making Process

From farm to your table, each step is crafted with precision and tradition

01
Step 1

Sugarcane Harvesting

Fresh sugarcane is carefully harvested from our organic farms at the perfect ripeness, ensuring maximum natural sweetness and nutritional value.

100% Organic Farms
02
Step 2

Juice Extraction

Using traditional crushing methods, we extract pure sugarcane juice without any chemical additives, preserving all natural minerals and vitamins.

Chemical-Free Process
03
Step 3

Traditional Boiling

The juice is slow-cooked in large traditional pots over controlled heat, allowing natural crystallization while maintaining the authentic flavor and aroma.

Time-Tested Methods
04
Step 4

Cooling & Solidifying

The concentrated mixture is poured into molds and allowed to cool naturally, forming solid blocks of pure jaggery with perfect consistency and texture.

Natural Cooling Process
05
Step 5

Packaging & Delivery

Each block is carefully inspected, hygienically packaged, and prepared for delivery to ensure it reaches you fresh with all its natural goodness intact.

Hygienic Packaging Standards
