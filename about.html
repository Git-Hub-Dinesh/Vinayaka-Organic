Our Story A Legacy of Pure Tradition

Vinayaga Exports was founded with a simple mission: to preserve the traditional art of jaggery making and bring pure, natural sweetness to every household. For generations, our family has been perfecting the craft of producing the finest quality jaggery.

What started as a small family business has now grown into a trusted name in Vinayaga jaggery Exports. Our commitment to quality, authenticity, and customer satisfaction has helped us build lasting relationships with clients worldwide.

"Every block of jaggery we produce carries the essence of tradition, the warmth of nature, and the promise of purity."
