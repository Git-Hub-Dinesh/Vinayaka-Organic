How to Use Our Jaggery

In Beverages

Perfect natural sweetener for tea, coffee, milk, and traditional drinks. Dissolves easily and adds rich flavor.

In Cooking & Baking

Ideal for sweets, desserts, and traditional recipes. Enhances flavor in cakes, cookies, and Indian sweets.

Ayurvedic Remedies

Used in traditional medicine with ginger, pepper, or herbs for cough, cold, and digestive issues.

Daily Nutrition

Replace white sugar in your daily diet for better health. Rich in iron, magnesium, and potassium.

In Snacks & Savories

Blend jaggery with roasted nuts, puffed rice, or sesame seeds to make healthy snacks like chikki or laddu. Adds a delicious crunch and natural sweetness.
